About Indus Spices

Leading Exporter of Authentic Indian Spices

Indus Spices is a trusted name in the global spice trade, specializing in the export of premium quality Indian spices. With over 15 years of experience, we've built strong partnerships with farmers across India's prime spice-growing regions.

Our state-of-the-art processing facilities and stringent quality control ensure that every shipment meets international food safety standards. From farm to container, we maintain complete traceability and transparency.

Quality Control

Certified Excellence at Every Stage

From farm selection to final shipment, our rigorous quality control ensures consistency and safety.

Laboratory Testing

In-house and third-party lab testing for microbiological safety, pesticide residues, heavy metals, moisture content, and volatile oil percentage.

  • E.coli, Salmonella testing
  • Aflatoxin analysis
  • Pesticide residue screening

Processing Standards

Modern processing facilities with automated cleaning, sorting, grinding, and metal detection equipment maintaining international food safety protocols.

  • Metal detector screening
  • Hygienic grinding units
  • Color sorting technology

Traceability

Complete batch tracking from farm to container. Every shipment includes detailed documentation with harvest dates, processing records, and test certificates.

  • Farm origin documentation
  • Batch-wise QC reports
  • Processing timeline records
